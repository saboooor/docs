Dimensions is a Minecraft server plugin that lets you create fully customized portals — built from any block material — that teleport players between worlds or even across BungeeCord/Velocity networks. Design your portals visually with the online Portal Creator, then drop the generated config file into your server to go live instantly.

## How Dimensions Works

Dimensions recreates the familiar nether portal experience using any block you choose. You build a portal frame, right-click with the configured lighter item to ignite it, and players can step through to travel to a destination world. An exit portal is automatically built at the destination so players can return.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Drop `Dimensions.jar` into your server's `plugins/` folder and restart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design your portal">
    Visit the [Portal Creator](https://astaspasta.alwaysdata.net/) to choose frame blocks, portal appearance, particle colors, and destination world. Download the generated `.yml` config.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the portal config">
    Place the downloaded `.yml` file in `./plugins/Dimensions/Portals/` and restart your server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build and light it in-game">
    Gather the required frame blocks, build the portal shape, and right-click with the lighter item to activate it. Your portal is now live!
  </Step>
</Steps>
