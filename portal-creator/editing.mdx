---
title: "Editing Your Portal in the Portal Creator: Full Guide"
sidebarTitle: "Editing"
description: "Click the interactive circles on the portal preview to configure the frame block, inside block, lighter material, and particle effects for your portal."
---

The Portal Creator's central preview displays your portal with several clickable circles overlaid on it. Each circle opens a dedicated configuration menu for that part of the portal. You can edit the frame block, the inside block, the lighter item, and the particle effect — all without writing any YAML.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Portal Creator">
    Navigate to [https://astaspasta.alwaysdata.net/](https://astaspasta.alwaysdata.net/) in your browser. The portal preview loads automatically in the center of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the frame block">
    Click the circle in the **top-left area of the portal** to open the frame block menu.

    Inside this menu you can:

    - **Set the accepted block face** — Restrict the frame to only accept a block placed along a specific axis. For example, you can require an acacia log placed along the Y axis.
    - **Set the minimum portal size** — Enter the minimum width and minimum height (in blocks) the portal frame must be.
    - **Set the maximum portal size** — Enter the maximum width and maximum height the portal frame can be.
    - **Choose a block** — Select a block from the list, or type a block name manually into the input field.

    <Note>
      If you type a block name that the editor doesn't recognise, it will display a placeholder image in the preview. The block name you entered still works correctly in-game — this is a display limitation of the editor only.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the inside block and lighter">
    Click the circle in the **center of the portal** to configure the inside block, or click the **lighter circle** to configure the lighter material.

    Both menus show a small box listing available blocks and items. Select one from the list, or type a name manually to use a block or item that isn't listed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure particles">
    Click the **particle element** on the portal preview to open the particle settings box.

    From here you can:

    - **Enable or disable particles** — Toggle whether the portal displays ambient particles at all.
    - **Change the particle color** — Use the color picker to choose any color for the portal's particles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Work through the frame block settings first (size constraints and block face), then move on to the inside block and lighter. Particles are purely cosmetic, so you can adjust those last.
</Tip>
