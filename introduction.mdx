Dimensions is a Spigot and Paper plugin that brings fully customizable portals to your Minecraft server. Instead of being locked into the vanilla nether portal look, you can craft portals out of any block material you choose, set a custom lighter item, and send players to any world on your server — or even to a completely different server. Whether you're running a survival network, an adventure map, or a creative hub, Dimensions gives you the portal system to match your vision.

## Free Version and Addons

The core Dimensions plugin is free and covers everything you need to get custom portals running on your server. If you want to go further, there are two addon options available:

- **Free addons pack** — extra portal behaviors and utilities at no cost, available on [SpigotMC](https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/dimensionsfreeaddons.97040/).
- **Paid addons pack** — the full suite of advanced features, available on [SpigotMC](https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/dimensions-custom-portals-full.83982/).

## Portal Creator Web Tool

Dimensions comes paired with an online **Portal Creator** tool at [https://astaspasta.alwaysdata.net/](https://astaspasta.alwaysdata.net/). Use it to visually configure your portal's frame block, lighter item, and destination, then download a ready-to-use `.yml` config file that you drop straight into your server. No manual YAML editing required.
